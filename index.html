SIMPLICITY

There’s something innately satisfying in living somewhere inherently beautiful. That’s The SKYRISE. With clean lines inspired by Italian architecture, It stands as an elegant addition to Denver’s constantly evolving landscape.



  • Fully integrated 1 and 2 bedroom apartments
  • Two carefully selected, design forward finish packages
  • Walking distance to City Park and a variety of restaurants on 17th


INNOVATION

The SKYRISE proves innovation is a mindset. That’s why it features the latest technologies integrated into every floor plan. It's smart home features give you the best of connection and convenience.



  • Home Automation System included in every unit
  • Full control of your home remotely from your mobile device
  • Entry, security, lighting and climate control




RIGHT WHERE YOU WANT TO BE


Nestled in the heart of Kigali's very best, The SKYRISE is positioned where you want to be, so you can spend more time being in the moments that matter

A walking distance from Kigali Heights, KBC, and Kimihurura Garden and close proximity to Simba Supermarket Gishushu.
